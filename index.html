I am currently a senior algorithm engineer in the Y-tech Graphics AI Team at Kuaishou Technology. In June 2023, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computer science from the Data Intelligence System Research Center at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Xueqi Cheng. In June 2017, I received my B.S. degree in computer science from the School of Computer Science at Sichuan University. My current research interests include deep generative models and image/video generation.

We are looking for self-motivated interns at Kuaishou Technology. Please feel free to contact me via email if you are interested.
